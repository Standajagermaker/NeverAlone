# NeverAlone Brand

## Name

NeverAlone

## Slogan

Never travel alone.

## Mission

Help people connect with real people while traveling.

## Brand idea

NeverAlone is built around a simple emotion: being in a new city should feel exciting, not lonely.

## Values

- Trust
- Safety
- Openness
- Adventure
- Authenticity
- Human connection

## Tone of voice

- Friendly
- Human
- Optimistic
- Clear
- Reassuring

Avoid:

- Overly romantic language
- Dating-only positioning
- Corporate jargon
- Fear-based language

## Primary message

> Meet travelers nearby for coffee, dinner, drinks, sightseeing or your next adventure.

## Secondary messages

- Find people nearby who are free right now.
- Chat with your AI Travel Companion while you wait.
- Meet real people. Share real moments.
- Discover places with someone, not alone.

## Visual direction

Style:

- Minimal
- Modern
- Warm
- Trustworthy
- Travel-inspired

Possible logo concepts:

- Compass plus two connected dots
- Map pin plus people symbol
- Minimal N mark
- Two paths meeting

## Color direction

Initial palette:

- Deep Blue: trust, night travel, depth
- White: clarity
- Emerald Accent: active, available, alive
- Warm Sand: travel, friendliness

## Typography

Suggested:

- Inter
- SF Pro for Apple-like native feel

## App icon concept

A simple symbol that communicates connection and location without looking like a dating app.

Options:

1. Two map pins connected by a curved path.
2. A compass needle shaped like an N.
3. Two dots meeting inside a circle.

## Store name options

- NeverAlone
- NeverAlone - Meet Travelers
- NeverAlone - Travel Social
- NeverAlone - Travel Companion

Preferred:

**NeverAlone - Meet Travelers**
