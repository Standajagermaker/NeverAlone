# NeverAlone Business Model

## Positioning

NeverAlone is a social travel companion, not a dating app.

Core message:

> Never travel alone.

## User problem

Solo travelers often arrive in a city and want a simple way to find someone nearby for coffee, dinner, drinks, sightseeing or a short shared activity.

Existing travel apps are often focused on booking, itineraries, hotels, flights or broad communities. NeverAlone focuses on live human connection.

## Main use cases

- I am alone tonight and want dinner with someone.
- I want to meet another traveler nearby for drinks.
- I want a travel partner for a city walk or day trip.
- I am waiting and want to talk to AI until someone replies.
- I need help translating or planning a local activity.

## Free plan

- Create profile
- Set availability
- Discover nearby users
- Basic chat
- Limited AI Companion
- Basic reporting and blocking

## Premium plan

Possible premium features:

- Unlimited AI Companion
- AI Concierge
- Advanced filters
- Profile boost
- Incognito mode
- Read receipts
- Unlimited translation
- Verified traveler badge

## Revenue streams

Primary:

- Monthly subscription
- Annual subscription

Future:

- Travel partnerships
- Event partnerships
- Premium verified meetups
- Local experience marketplace

## Launch strategy

Start with one or two cities where solo travel and digital nomads are common.

Candidate cities:

- Barcelona
- Berlin
- Prague
- Lisbon
- Madrid
- Amsterdam

## Growth loops

- Users invite people they meet while traveling.
- Activity cards are shareable.
- AI generates friendly invite messages.
- Verified users create trust.

## Metrics

Core metrics:

- New users
- Activated users
- Users with active availability
- Nearby matches
- Messages sent
- Meetings confirmed
- Reports per 1,000 users
- Retention after 1 day, 7 days, 30 days

## Risks

- Safety concerns
- Fake profiles
- Low density in early cities
- App store review requirements
- Location privacy

## Risk mitigation

- Launch city by city
- Approximate location only
- Reporting and blocking from day one
- Clear positioning away from dating-only use
- Strong onboarding around safety
