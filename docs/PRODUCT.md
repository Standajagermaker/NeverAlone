# NeverAlone Product Document

**Slogan:** *Never travel alone.*

## Vision

NeverAlone helps travelers, digital nomads and locals meet real people nearby for coffee, dinner, drinks, sightseeing or shared adventures. It is not a dating app; it is a social travel companion focused on reducing loneliness while traveling.

## Mission

Nobody should have to travel alone if they do not want to.

## Target users

- Travelers
- Backpackers
- Digital nomads
- Solo travelers
- Locals who enjoy meeting visitors

## Core value proposition

Open the app, choose what you want to do right now, and find people nearby who want the same.

Examples:

- Coffee
- Beer
- Wine
- Dinner
- Museum
- Beach
- Hiking
- Travel Partner
- Nightlife

## MVP features

### Authentication

- Email
- Google
- Apple

### Profile

- Profile photo
- Name
- Age
- Country
- Languages
- Short bio
- Interests
- Verification status
- Rating

### Nearby

- GPS-based discovery
- Adjustable radius: 500 m, 2 km, 5 km, 10 km
- Approximate location only

### Chat

- Text messages
- Photos
- Emojis
- Location sharing
- AI-assisted translation

### AI Travel Companion

Available whenever no suitable people are nearby or while the user waits for a reply.

Capabilities:

- Friendly conversation
- Local recommendations
- Translation help
- Safety tips
- Trip planning
- Suggestions to improve matching

### Trust and safety

- Email verification
- Phone verification
- Reporting
- Blocking
- Hidden exact location
- Future selfie verification
