# NeverAlone

**Never travel alone.**

NeverAlone is a social travel companion app that helps travelers, solo travelers, backpackers, digital nomads and locals meet real people nearby for coffee, dinner, drinks, sightseeing or shared adventures.

It is not positioned as a dating app. The core mission is simple:

> Nobody should have to travel alone if they do not want to.

## Product pillars

- **Real People** - discover people nearby who are available now.
- **Live Activities** - choose what you want to do: coffee, dinner, drinks, museum, beach, hiking, nightlife or travel partner.
- **AI Travel Companion** - chat with AI while waiting, get local tips, translation help and safety guidance.
- **Trust & Safety** - verification, reporting, blocking and approximate location by design.
